About Naruto

Series Overview

Description of Naruto series...

Notable Themes

  • Friendship and Teamwork
  • Overcoming Adversity
  • Personal Growth

Key Facts

Original Run: 2002-2017

Creator: Masashi Kishimoto

Genres: Action, Adventure, Fantasy

Character Profiles

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto is a ninja with a dream to become the strongest leader of his village.

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke is a skilled and stoic ninja who seeks to avenge his clan.

Sakura Haruno

Sakura is a talented ninja with exceptional strength and medical skills.

Fun facts and historical context about the Naruto series...

Episode Guides

Episode 1: Naruto's Ninja Way

In this episode, Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with a dream of becoming the strongest ninja, faces various challenges and starts his journey with his first mission.

Manga Chapters

Chapter 1: Naruto Uzumaki

The first chapter introduces Naruto Uzumaki, his background, and his aspirations to become the Hokage, the leader of his village.

This chapter delves into Naruto's relationship with his village and the expectations placed upon him as the Hokage's son.
